Hello! I’m a Colombian economist living in New York City. I’m interested in macroeconomic development research, particularly on labor economics and climate change.

I hold a B.A. and a M.A. in Economics from Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia.

Currently, I work as a Pre-doctoral Research Assistant at the Economics Division of Columbia Business School. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Research Department of the Inter-American Development Bank, as a Research Assistant at the Center for Sustainable Finance at Universidad de los Andes.
